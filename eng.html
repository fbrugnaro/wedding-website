Schedule

Guests arrival

The guests are expected to enter the venue at 5:30 PM. Upon their arrival, they will have the opportunity to use the parking located within the castle.

Civil ceremony

The civil wedding ceremony will begin at 6:00 PM. Immediately afterwards, there will be a photo session with relatives and friends, which will continue for the rest of the evening.

Reception

After the ceremony concludes, we will proceed with the first part of the reception, characterized by live music and an appetizer buffet.

Dinner

Subsequently, dinner will take place in the Angelica Hall. Finally, we will move to the pool area to enjoy desserts and celebrate the cutting of the wedding cake.
